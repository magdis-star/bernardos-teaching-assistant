# Bernardo's Teaching Assistant - Landing Page

## Project Overview
Landing page to recruit senior English learners (55+) for a PhD research study testing an AI-powered English conversation practice app.

**Target Audience:** Spanish speakers aged 55+ learning English
**Purpose:** Recruitment for research study participants
**Languages:** Bilingual (English/Spanish)

---

## Design Reference
See `design-mockup.png` for visual direction

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Soft sage green (#7CB5A3 approx)
- Background: White
- Text: Navy blue
- Accent: Blue for CTAs

**Design Principles:**
- Large, readable typography for seniors
- High contrast for accessibility
- Clean, uncluttered layout
- Simple navigation
- Prominent, clear call-to-action buttons

## Content (Provided by Client)

### English Version

**Hero Section:**
- Headline: "Practice English Conversation Anytime—At Your Own Pace, Without Pressure"
- Subheading: "An AI-powered English conversation tutor designed for learners aged 55+, offering naturally adaptive dialogues wherever you are, whenever you're ready."
- CTA: "Request Your Invitation"

**How It Works:**
Three-step process:
1. **Simply Start Talking** - No buttons to press—just speak naturally
2. **Get Personalized Support** - AI detects your skill level and adjusts
3. **Practice Real Conversations** - Work, travel, family topics

**Why This Works for You:**
- No pressure to keep up
- Speaks at a comfortable pace
- Available 24/7 on your phone
- Remembers your progress
- Designed specifically for older adults

**Who We're Looking For:**
- Age 55 or older
- Learning English (any level welcome)
- Own a smartphone (iPhone or Android)
- Live in or near Madrid, Spain
- Willing to try the app for 4 weeks

**What You'll Get:**
- Free access to the app
- Personal onboarding session
- Ongoing support throughout the study
- Contribution to PhD research on senior language learning

### Spanish Version

**Hero Section:**
- Headline: "Practica Conversación en Inglés Cuando Quieras—A Tu Ritmo, Sin Presión"

- Diseñado específicamente para adultos mayores

**A Quién Buscamos:**

**Qué Recibirás:**
- Acceso gratuito a la aplicación

- Apoyo continuo durante el estudio
- Contribución a investigación doctoral sobre aprendizaje de idiomas en mayores

- High contrast ratios
- Clear focus indicators
- Keyboard navigation
- Screen reader optimization
